Thank you for purchasing a Digital Leather Business Card!

Digital Leather Business Cards leverage NFC (Near Field Communication) technology to allow you to instantly share your contact information, website, social media, and more with a simple tap to a compatible smartphone. This guide will walk you through encoding a URL onto your Digital Leather Business Card using the NFC Tools mobile app.

What you'll need:

  • Your Digital Leather business card (which contains an NFC chip)
  • A smartphone with NFC capabilities (most modern smartphones have this)
  • The NFC Tools mobile app

Download the NFC Tools app:

  • For iOS (iPhone): NFC Tools on the App Store
  • For Android: NFC Tools - Apps on Google Play

Instructions for Setting Your URL

  1. Open the NFC Tools app on your smartphone.
  2. Navigate to the "Write" tab in the app.
  3. Tap on "Add a record."
  4. From the list of options, select "URL / URI."
  5. Enter the full URL you wish to encode onto your business card (e.g., https://www.yourwebsite.com, https://linkedin.com/in/yourprofile).
  6. Tap "Validate" (or a similar confirmation button) to confirm the URL.
  7. Tap on "Write / X Bytes" (where 'X' represents the size of the data to be written).
  8. Hold your Digital Leather business card close to the NFC antenna of your smartphone. The location of the NFC antenna can vary:
    • iPhones: Typically at the very top center of the phone.
    • Android Phones: Usually in the middle or upper-middle back of the phone.
  9. Keep the card still until you receive a confirmation message from the NFC Tools app indicating that the writing was successful.

Testing Your Digital Leather Business Card

  1. Once encoded, you can test your card by going back to the main menu of the NFC Tools app and selecting "Read."
  2. Hold your Digital Leather business card to your phone's NFC antenna again.
  3. The app should now display the URL you encoded. You can also test it by simply tapping your card to another NFC-enabled smartphone (without the NFC Tools app open) to see if the URL opens automatically in a web browser.

How to Use Your Digital Leather Business Card

Now that your Digital Leather business card is encoded, sharing your information is incredibly easy!

  1. Simply tap your Digital Leather business card to a compatible smartphone.
  2. Ensure the recipient's phone has NFC enabled (most newer smartphones have this on by default).
  3. The optimal tapping location on a phone varies:
    • iPhones: Tap the top edge of your Digital Leather card to the very top center of the iPhone.
    • Android Phones: Tap the center of your Digital Leather card to the middle or upper-middle back of the Android phone.
  4. A notification will pop up on the recipient's phone (often at the top of the screen). Tapping this notification will open the URL you encoded directly in their web browser, instantly sharing your information!

You have now successfully encoded your URL onto your Digital Leather business card!
